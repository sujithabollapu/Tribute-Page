Tribute To Rohit Sharma

About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. His parents are Gurunath Sharma, who worked as a caretaker in a transport firm, and Purnima Sharma, a housewife from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Despite facing financial difficulties, his parents supported his cricketing ambitions, and he was later raised by his grandparents in Borivali, Mumbai.
